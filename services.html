RENTING A CAR

When renting a car with Kingslandcar, you’re given several options from which to choose. If you’re hoping to travel in style and need a chic ride for your weekend rental, opt for a convertible, a luxury vehicle or one of our exotic models. If you’re in a large group and need a long-term rental for people and luggage, book a passenger van, minivan or SUV. If you’ll be town-hopping, rent a sedan, or even a compact or economy car. Whichever vehicle you choose, Kingslandcar has bookable extras for your trip like GPS, child seats and an additional driver.
